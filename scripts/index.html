About Me

Hi, welcome. I am Rui, a PhD student at Aalto University, supervised by Prof. Arno Solin.

My research interests include Bayesian deep learning, uncertainty quantification, probabilistic machine learning, and few-shot learning.

Previously, I obtained my Master degree in Machine Learning from University College London (UCL) and Bachelor degree in Physics from Sun Yat-sen University.
